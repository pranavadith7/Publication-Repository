About Us!


Welcome to Faculty Publication Repository. "An electronic repository is considered to preserve, host, manage and facilitate the sharing of intellectual productivity of institutional faculty". This Institutional Repository has been created to collect, preserve and distribute the scholarly output of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore. This will work as an important tool to facilitate scholarly communication and preserve the institution knowledge. Institutional repositories enable researchers to self-archive their research output and can improve the visibility, usage and impact of research conducted at an institution.



Services


This repository facilitates professors to upload their research papers, journals, datasets and about the conferences attended. The students can search and view papers accordingly. The students can also download papers for their future references. Faculty can build their profile and share about them.
